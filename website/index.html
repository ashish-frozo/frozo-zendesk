Frequently Asked Questions

How does PII detection work?

We use Microsoft Presidio, an industry-standard AI-powered detection engine, combined with spaCy NLP and custom patterns to detect 15+ types of sensitive information including names, emails, phone numbers, credit cards, and API keys.

Is it GDPR compliant?

Yes! We never store original PII - only redacted content. All OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest. Complete audit trail. Privacy by design. SOC 2 Type II certification in progress.

How long does setup take?

Installation takes less than 5 minutes. Simply install from Zendesk Marketplace, configure your Jira credentials, and you're ready to escalate safely.

Can I customize what gets redacted?

Pro and Enterprise plans include custom redaction patterns. Configure your own regex patterns or entity types to match your specific compliance needs.

What happens if detection fails?

You always review a preview before exporting. Low-confidence detections are flagged with warnings. You can cancel and manually sanitize if needed.

Do you support other ticketing systems?

Currently Zendesk only. ServiceNow and Freshdesk integrations are on our roadmap. Contact us if you need a specific integration.
